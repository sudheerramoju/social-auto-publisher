## ✅ CORRECT TERMS TO USE

**YES - Use These:**
- ✅ Web Service
- ✅ SaaS Platform
- ✅ Cloud-based Service
- ✅ Web Application
- ✅ Online Platform
- ✅ Social Media Management Tool
- ✅ Publishing Service
- ✅ API Integration Platform

**AVOID - Don't Use These:**
- ❌ "Just a website" (undersells it)
- ❌ "App" (confusing - web app vs mobile app)
- ❌ "Software" (implies download/install)
- ❌ "Bot" (sounds automated/spammy)
- ❌ "Script" (sounds unprofessional)
