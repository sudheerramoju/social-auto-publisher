## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Add a personal photo** - Posts with faces get 38% more engagement
2. **Keep it conversational** - Write like you're talking to a friend
3. **Ask a question** - Ends with question = more comments
4. **Respond fast** - First hour is critical for algorithm
5. **Tag relevant people** - If you used their tools, tag them
6. **Use line breaks** - Makes post easy to read
7. **Don't link immediately** - Comment your link for better reach
